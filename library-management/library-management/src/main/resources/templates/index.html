Welcome to Library Management System

At our Library Management System, we offer a seamless platform for managing books, categories, and customer requests. Whether you're an admin adding new books, managing categories, or approving customer requests, our system ensures smooth library operations. Customers can explore books and request them with ease. Join us today to efficiently manage and access your library's resources.

Features

Add Book Categories

Admins can easily add and manage different book categories, ensuring the library catalog is well-organized and accessible.

Manage Books

Efficiently manage the library's collection by adding new books, updating book details, and categorizing them for better searchability.

Customer Book Requests

Customers can explore the catalog and request books, while admins can approve or reject requests based on availability.
